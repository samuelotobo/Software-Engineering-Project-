The context diagram shows the Return Book System interacting with the Student, Library Database, Notification Service, and Shelf Mechanism. The Level 1 DFD decomposes the system into major processes such as validating books, checking due dates, processing fines, updating inventory, and sending notifications.

The Level 2 DFD further expands the Process Fine function into smaller sub-processes including fine calculation, presenting the fine to the student, and recording payment transactions.
